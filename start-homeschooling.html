Students with IEPs

If your child had an IEP before beginning homeschool, you have two options:

  1. Work with the school district to revoke / cancel the IEP.
  2. Keep services active and submit annual objectives for pre-approval.

I’ll gladly help you craft or approve objectives—free of charge—so special-needs families aren’t burdened with extra cost.
