Personalized Tutoring Services

We offer expert academic support tailored to your child’s needs, whether one-on-one or in a small group setting.

One-on-One Tutoring

Our individualized tutoring sessions provide focused attention on your child’s strengths and challenges. Perfect for deep diving into specific topics, reinforcing classroom learning, and building confidence.

Rate: $40 per hour

Small Group Tutoring

Group sessions foster collaborative learning among 2 to 5 students. Each learner receives personalized support within an interactive environment, making lessons engaging and affordable.

Rate: $20 per hour per student (2–5 students)

Subjects Covered

  • Reading, Writing & Spelling
  • Mathematics (Arithmetic, Algebra, Geometry)
  • Science & Humanities
  • Study Skills & Organization
  • Test Prep & Assessment Review

Benefits of Tutoring

  • Customized lesson plans to match learning pace
  • Stronger foundational skills and concept mastery
  • Improved academic confidence and motivation
  • Flexible scheduling to fit your family’s needs
  • Progress tracking and parent feedback
