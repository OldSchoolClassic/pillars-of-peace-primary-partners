About Pillars of Peace Primary Partners

A homeschool hybrid to help parents meet homeschool legal requirements. A place where teachers and parents witness together to raise a child up in the way he should go.

Parents and kids need and deserve a high quality, flexible, applicable education without interference from government mandates and social agendas. Our aim is to create such an option by focusing on helping parents meet legal homeschooling requirements in an efficient, semi-structured, yet flexible environment.

Our Mission

We guide children and parents through the homeschooling process from how to register through end-of-year evaluations and everything in between. We provide certified teachers with proven curriculums and strategies that work, especially for children who have fallen through the cracks of traditional schooling.

About Alona Hirsh

Hello! I’m Alona Hirsh, a mother of three and a certified elementary teacher with a Master’s in Special Education. I have homeschooled since 2016 and taught students of all ages since 2013.

I founded Pillars of Peace Primary Partners after hearing from parents overwhelmed by homeschool requirements and concerned about political agendas in curricula. I believe in a peaceful, encouraging environment where parents partner with teachers to support their child’s learning journey.
