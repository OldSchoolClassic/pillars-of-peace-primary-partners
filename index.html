About Us

Parents and kids deserve a high quality, flexible education without interference from mandates or agendas. We focus on helping parents meet PA homeschool requirements in an efficient, semi-structured, yet flexible environment where students learn essential life skills.

Why Choose Pillars of Peace

  • No busy work—focus on what truly matters
  • Certified teachers with special education expertise
  • Legal requirements simplified and stress-free
  • Supportive virtual community for parents and students
  • Customizable curriculum options that work

Our Mission

To guide children and parents through registration to end-of-year evaluations and beyond. We empower certified teachers to use proven strategies, especially for those who’ve fallen through the cracks of traditional schooling.

Meet Alona Hirsh

Hello! I’m Alona Hirsh, mother of three and certified elementary teacher with a Master’s in Special Education. I’ve homeschooled since 2016 and taught a range of ages and abilities since 2013.

I founded Pillars of Peace after hearing from parents who felt overwhelmed by legal requirements and curricula agendas. I believe in a peaceful, encouraging environment where parents partner with teachers to support every child’s journey.
