Prepare for Your Homeschool Evaluation

Everything you need to ensure a smooth, compliant process in Pennsylvania

Book Your Evaluation

Portfolio Samples

Include two or three samples for every subject (beginning, middle and end of year). Worksheets, artwork, reports, tests, projects—anything that shows growth.

Partial Year Samples

Switching to homeschool mid-year? Add early work from the original school to establish a clear baseline and highlight progress.

Required Subjects

Elementary (1-6)

  • English – spelling, reading, writing
  • Arithmetic & Math
  • Science
  • Social Studies & Civics
  • Health & Safety
  • PE • Music • Art

Secondary (7-12)

  • English – literature, composition
  • Science – biology, chemistry
  • Math – algebra, geometry (trig+)
  • Social Studies & Economics
  • PE • Music • Art
  • Foreign Language (recommended)

Reading Log

Track books read or heard. Younger learners typically log 30+ titles; older students record longer works or series.

Standardized Assessments

Grades 3, 5 & 8 include nationally normed test scores—any level that matches the student is acceptable.

Hours & Days

Elementary: 900 hrs / 180 days  |  Secondary: 990 hrs / 180 days. A signed statement of completion meets the requirement.

Quick Checklist

  • Portfolio samples
  • Reading log
  • Standardized test results
  • Payment – $40 (first) / $25 (sibling)
  • Bring your questions

Start-of-Year Forms

Submit Affidavit, Objectives (K-6 or 7-12) and medical/dental documents—or an exemption—by August 1.
