Prepare for Your Homeschool Evaluation

Everything you need to ensure a smooth, compliant process in Pennsylvania

Book Your Evaluation

Portfolio Samples

Include two or three samples per subject—beginning, middle and end of year. Worksheets, artwork, projects, tests—anything that shows growth.

Partial Year Samples

If you began homeschooling mid-year, add early work from the previous school to establish a baseline and highlight progress.

Required Subjects

Elementary (1-6)

  • English – spelling, reading, writing
  • Arithmetic & Math
  • Science
  • Social Studies & Civics
  • Health & Safety
  • PE • Music • Art

Secondary (7-12)

  • English – literature, composition
  • Science – biology, chemistry
  • Math – algebra, geometry, trig
  • Social Studies & Economics
  • PE • Music • Art
  • Foreign Language (recommended)

Reading Log

Track every book read or heard. Younger students typically list 30+ titles; older readers record longer works or series.

Standardized Assessments

Grades 3, 5 & 8 attach nationally normed test scores. The test level should match the student’s current ability—not necessarily their age-grade.

Hours & Days

Elementary: 900 hrs / 180 days  |  Secondary: 990 hrs / 180 days. A signed statement that you met the requirement is all the law demands.

Quick Checklist

  • ✓ Portfolio samples
  • ✓ Reading log
  • ✓ Standardized test results
  • ✓ $40 first child / $25 sibling
  • ✓ Your question list

Start-of-Year Forms

Submit Affidavit, Objectives (K-6 or 7-12) and medical/dental documents (or exemption) by August 1.
